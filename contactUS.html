Frequently asked questions

What certifications do you have to ensure the quality and safety of your seafood products?

We adhere to international standards such as HACCP, ISO, and BAP (Best Aquaculture Practices) to ensure our seafood is safe, high-quality, and meets global regulatory requirements.

How do you handle the freshness and storage of seafood during transportation?

We use advanced cold chain logistics to maintain optimal temperatures throughout the transportation process. Our seafood is stored in temperature-controlled containers to ensure freshness and prevent spoilage.

What is the typical lead time for orders, and how do you handle delays?

The typical lead time for orders is 7-14 business days, depending on the destination. In case of delays due to customs, weather, or other factors, we provide real-time updates and work closely with logistics partners to resolve issues promptly.

Do you offer customized packaging and labeling for international markets?

Yes, we provide customized packaging and labeling to meet the specific requirements of international markets, including compliance with local regulations and language preferences.

What is your process for ensuring traceability of seafood products?

We use advanced traceability systems to track the origin of our seafood, including where and how it was caught or farmed. This ensures transparency and compliance with international standards.

How do you handle customs clearance and documentation for international shipments?

Our team assists with all customs clearance and documentation, ensuring compliance with international trade regulations. We handle tariffs, permits, and other paperwork to ensure smooth delivery.

What is your policy for handling damaged or spoiled shipments?

In the rare event of damaged or spoiled shipments, we offer refunds, replacements, or compensation based on the extent of the issue. Our priority is to ensure customer satisfaction.

Can you provide references or testimonials from other clients in my region?

Yes, we can provide references and testimonials from clients in your region to demonstrate our reliability and reputation in the seafood export and import industry.
